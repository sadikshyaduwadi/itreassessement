INTERNATIONAL DESTINATION

DUBAI

If you’re looking for the perfect destination to tie the knot, look no further than Dubai! With a variety of unique wedding locations, it’s no wonder this city is one of the top destinations in the world when it comes to celebrating your nuptials. Dubai has so many amazing wedding venues. The vastness of the Arabian desert is one of the best settings for a wedding ever. With an endless horizon, camel rides through valleys and sparkling blue lakes, etc.


• Best Time for Wedding In Dubai - September - April

THAILAND

It’s a place of beauty, serenity, and love. Nowhere does all that better than Thailand. The country is renowned for its ancient temples, lush rainforests, and tropical beaches. Looking to tie the knot in Thailand? You’re in luck… it is one of the best places to get married in this beautiful country. From beachside resorts to rooftop cocktail bars, there are so many marriage venues to choose from. Thailand is a country bursting with natural beauty. This picturesque nation is home to tropical rainforest, idyllic sandy beaches, and towering mountain peaks. Moreover, the country’s relaxed culture and easygoing people make it an ideal location for a honeymoon.


• The Best Time for Weddings in Thailand - is November to May

GOA

Goa is a beautiful state in India, said to be one of the most romantic spots on earth. The beaches are gorgeous and the local cuisine is some of the best you will ever experience. So if you’re looking to tie the knot in Goa, then we’ve got some great recommendations for you. Weddings in Goa are considered to be among the most elaborate and expensive celebrations in the state. This state has a rich cultural tradition that is reflected in the pan-Indian festivities of marriages it is also known as the wedding capital of India, and with its breathtaking coastal views, lush green forests, and hilly terrains, Goa is one perfect place for a honeymoon.


• Best Time for Wedding in Goa- November - March

BALI

Bali is one of the best places to get hitched. It’s an incredibly romantic island, and it has a reputation for being an ideal honeymoon destination. With hundreds of idyllic beaches, lush forests, and picturesque rice fields, Bali offers visitors a plethora of stunning locations to tie the knot. When you’re planning your perfect Destination wedding in Bali, you’ll be able to pick from a wide range of venues. From sprawling hotels to gorgeous villas, there is something for every style and budget in Bali as a wedding destination. The Balinese have also embraced modern concepts in wedding planning and have adapted to accommodate all kinds of couples who wish to tie the knot on this beautiful island.


• Best Time for a wedding in Bali – May-August

ITALY

If you’re planning to tie the knot, Italy is one of the most magical places in the world. With stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage, this little country packs an unbelievable amount of jaw-dropping experiences for those looking to get hitched. Italy is a renowned city since it is the wedding destination of Celebrities Like Virat Kohli – Anushka Sharma and Ranveer Singh –Deepika Padukone. From hilltop restaurants in the heart of medieval towns to horse-drawn carriage rides through vineyards, Italy offers a vast array of wedding venues. From elegant opera houses to hidden gardens and lakeside pavilions, there is something for every type of wedding. If you’re looking for a destination with a history of marriage, then look no further than Italy.


• Best time for Wedding – May - September
