About Sky High ....

The special moments in our lives are meant to be perfect. At Sky High,
we strive to bring your dreams to life. Since 2010, we have been working
with a wide range of clients for various events and have gathered valuable
experiences along the way. Now, after thriving in the industry for more than
a decade, we are now launching as a professional event organizer.

OUR VISION

Sky High Events strives to reduce the stress of wedding planning for the busy, sophisticated couple and create a unique and extraordinary experience for each one.

OUR MISSION

At Sky High Events, we make it our mission to make your wedding or event planning as enjoyable as possible. We truly believe that you should enjoy the special moments in your life and have as little stress as possible in the process. Our experienced coordinators will create memories to last a lifetime.
